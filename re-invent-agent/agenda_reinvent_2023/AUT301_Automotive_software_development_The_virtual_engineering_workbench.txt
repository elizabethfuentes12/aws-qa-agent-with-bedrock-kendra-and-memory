description: <p>Have you wondered how to build and test automotive-grade vehicle functions on AWS? In this workshop, see how with the Virtual Engineering Workbench (VEW). Learn to create preconfigured AUTOSAR and QNX runtime environments and publish them in the AWS Service Catalog. Then, from the VEW self-service portal, select and log in to the preconfigured AUTOSAR and QNX environments to build a demo vehicle function application. Learn to integrate and run the vehicle applications on virtualized targets (virtual ECUs), running on Amazon EC2 instances, to verify and test functionality. Explore the end-to-end vision of the VEW, extending and customizing it to support your requirements and workflows. You must bring your laptop to participate.</p>
